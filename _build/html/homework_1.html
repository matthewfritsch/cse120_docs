Getting Started on HW1¶

How to connect remotely to the bohr1 server¶

  • Get your SSH client open

  • Connect to the unix timeshare. On a terminal, this can be ssh (cruz ID)@unix.ucsc.edu

  • Once on the unix timeshare, ssh into the bohr1 server with ssh cse120_(group number)@bohr1.soe.ucsc.edu

  • You’re now connected!
